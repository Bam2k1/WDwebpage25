Volkswagen. A German car manufacturer company established in 1937, who are also one of the largest car manufacturers in the world. Volkswagen are known for producing passenger cars and commercial vehicles.

Manual vehicles are becoming less and less common with the advancement of technology, it is seen as an outdated method of controlling your vehicle. Despite this, it allows for the driver to be in complete control and be part of the cog in the machine.

Volkswagen cater their vehicles for comfortability as well as sportiness. They try to appeal to the widest range of consumers, which makes the driving experience of their vehicles to be pure bliss.

The Volkswagen GTI, released in 2006 was a high-performance hatchback that was the epitomy of the german manufacturers sporty comfort mindset. Produced with a turbocharged engine that can easily reach over 300 horsepower and features that boast their superior engineering skills.

"Stancing" is a common trend for car enthusiasts that consists of owners lowering their vehicle with aftermarket wheels. Their objective is to get the best fitment, with the wheel as close to the body of the car as possible. Though this requires maticulous work it is a popular trend for people who own Volkswagen due to the cheap price point and accessibility.
